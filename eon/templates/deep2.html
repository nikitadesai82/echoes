A Living Landscape
Welcome to Echoes of Nature, an interactive experience that reflects the House of Hiranandani’s vision of harmony between modern living and the natural environment. Located within the ecological corridor between Thane Creek and Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Hiranandani Estate, Thane is a 375 acre green haven supporting a rich diversity of plants, trees, birds, butterflies, and small wildlife; made possible through thoughtful landscaping, preservation of native species, intentional creation of green spaces and revitalization of existing water bodies.

Echoes of Nature invites visitors to discover the stories of tree-shaded walkways, butterfly-filled gardens, and birds nesting by the lake, highlighting the House of Hiranandani’s commitment to protecting biodiversity and helping people reconnect with nature every day.
