A Living Landscape
Welcome to Echoes of Nature, an interactive experience that reflects the House of Hiranandaniâ€™s vision of harmony between modern living and the natural environment. Located within the ecological corridor between Thane Creek and Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Hiranandani Estate, Thane is a 375 acre green haven supporting a rich diversity of plants, trees, birds, butterflies and small wildlife; made possible through thoughtful landscaping, preservation of native species, intentional creation of green spaces and revitalization of existing water bodies.

Echoes of Nature invites visitors to discover the stories of tree-shaded walkways, butterfly-filled gardens and birds nesting by the lake, highlighting the House of Hiranandaniâ€™s commitment to protecting biodiversity and helping people reconnect with nature every day.
WANT TO KNOW
WHO'S CHIRPING?
Meet the colourful, feathered residents
and discover more about them.
There are over 40 different varieties of birds to be seen almost every day at Hiranandani Estate, Thane. Come learn which of these birds is awake at night, which eat fish or drink nectar and who has travelled thousands of miles to spend the winter here.
COME MEET
THE GREEN GIANTS!
Get to know the leafy locals, from shady trees to beautiful blooms and amazing herbs.
Figure out which trees bloom in summer, which fruit is loved by bats and which barks and flowers can be used for medicine. Along the way, you will also discover some unique and exotic species that have been carefully nurtured and now flourish.
GREEN VISIONARY
Surendra Hiranandani
Chairman and Managing Director
Driven by a lifelong passion for horticulture, Mr. Surendra Hiranandani has played a hands-on role in researching and selecting the plant species best suited to the soil and climate of Hiranandani Estate, Thane thus shaping the landscape into a thriving oasis of beauty and sustainability.
