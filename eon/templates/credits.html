Credits & Usage Notice
About This Experience
Echoes of Nature is an educational, interactive experience developed as part of the House of Hiranandani’s Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiative. It is intended solely for public awareness, environmental education, and community engagement, and is accessible free of charge. No commercial promotion, sales activity, or monetization is associated with this kiosk or its corresponding website.
Use of Creative Commons–Licensed Material
This experience incorporates bird call recordings sourced from xeno-canto.org, a global community archive of bird sounds, shared under the Creative Commons Attribution–NonCommercial–ShareAlike 4.0 International License (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0). These recordings are used strictly for non-commercial, educational display and interpretation as part of this CSR initiative. Where recordings have been edited (trimmed, normalised, or looped) for exhibit purposes, the adapted versions are shared under the same CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 license, in accordance with ShareAlike requirements.
